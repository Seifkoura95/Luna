How Luna Points Work

Earn 25% back at every Luna Group venue. Every dollar you spend, every mission you complete, every milestone you hit — Luna Points stack, and they redeem for real dollar value inside the app.

The Rate 10 PTS = $1 SPEND

$1 spent → 10 Luna Points → $0.25 redemption value

Effective cashback: 25% of every dollar you spend comes back to you in redeemable Luna Points. Silver members earn 2× (20 pts/$1), Gold members earn 3× (30 pts/$1).

When & How Points Are Awarded

There are three ways to earn Luna Points, and each has a different flow — designed to prevent any kind of gaming or abuse:

1. In-app actions (instant)

  • Subscribing to Luna+ (Silver/Gold) — points credited the moment your subscription activates
  • Completing missions — points credited the instant all criteria pass server-side verification
  • Hitting milestones — tier unlock rewards credited automatically

2. Venue purchases (post-verification at the venue)

When you buy something at a Luna venue — bottle service, auction items, tables, or anything the staff rings up — points are awarded only after the full transaction is verified by a staff member at the venue on the night.

  • You place the order (or win the auction) through the app
  • Stripe charges your deposit, not the full amount
  • At the venue, the staff member confirms what you actually consumed via the Venue Staff Portal
  • Staff enter the final dollar amount → points are awarded at 10 pts / $1 (or your multiplier)
Why this matters: This prevents people from ordering a $2,000 bottle service in the app, paying the $200 deposit, and walking out with 20,000 fake points. Points are tied to real consumption, verified at the bar.

3. Check-ins (venue-verified)

Walking into a Luna venue via the app's geofence earns small "show up" bonus points, but these require the staff or QR scanner at the door to mark you present. Location alone isn't enough.

Anti-abuse controls on every mission

  • Server-side-only state: mission progress lives in MongoDB, never trusted from the client
  • Geofence + time-stamp: check-ins require GPS match + current time window
  • Idempotency keys: each check-in has a unique server ID; replaying requests does nothing
  • Staff override: a staff member can revoke points awarded on fraudulent missions via the admin panel
  • Rate limits: 1 check-in per venue per 4 hours per user
  • Account binding: check-ins require an active auth token tied to a verified email & phone

How milestone rewards are protected

  • Single-use QR codes: each reward generates a unique QR. Once scanned at the venue, it's marked redeemed in the database and can never be scanned again — even if screenshot-shared
  • Server-side redemption: the app shows the QR but the redemption state lives on our servers. Taking a screenshot of a used QR and showing it to another bar = won't work, the scan returns "already redeemed"
  • Milestone unlock is lifetime: once you hit 5,000 lifetime points, you're Luna Elite forever — redeeming doesn't "un-unlock" you
  • Staff confirmation on major perks: booth reservations, free entries, etc. show up in the Venue Staff Portal with the user's name + photo on the night

Preventing Abuse — The Short Version

Luna Group uses 6 layers of defence so that points always reflect real spend or real visits:

  1. Nothing user-reported: users never "submit" what they spent. Only staff or verified sensors (geofence, Stripe webhook) can trigger point awards.
  2. Stripe is the source of truth for money: points from purchases only flow after payment_intent.succeeded or staff confirmation of in-venue spend.
  3. Server-side idempotency: same action replayed 100 times = 1 award.
  4. QR tickets are single-use: once scanned, flipped to status: redeemed server-side instantly.
  5. Rate limiting: check-ins, mission progress, and referrals are throttled at the user + IP + venue level.
  6. Staff revoke panel: if fraud is detected, points can be clawed back in the admin panel — users see an entry in their activity feed explaining why.
