Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 February 2026 · Effective immediately

In plain English: We collect the info we need to run your Luna+ membership — your name, email, phone, spend history, and location when you check in at our venues. We use Stripe for payments and never store your card details. We don't sell your data. You can delete your account anytime from the app.

1. Who we are

This policy describes how Luna Group Hospitality Pty Ltd (ABN on request, "Luna Group", "we", "us", "our") handles personal information collected through the Luna Group mobile app, our website lunagroupapp.com.au, and our venues (Eclipse, Kenjin, After Dark, JuJu's, Night Market, Ember & Ash and any future Luna venues).

We are bound by the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). For users in the EU/UK we also observe the GDPR/UK-GDPR where it applies to them.

2. Who can use the app

Luna Group is a nightlife and hospitality membership service for adults. You must be at least 18 years old (the lawful drinking age in Queensland, Australia) to create an account or use the Luna Group app. We will delete any account we identify as belonging to a person under 18 and refund any unused subscription fees.

3. What we collect

3.1 You give us directly

  • Account info — full name, email address, mobile phone number, date of birth, password (stored only as a salted bcrypt hash).
  • Profile info — optional display name, profile photo, gender, preferences you select (favourite venue, music genres).
  • Support messages — anything you tell us through in-app chat, email to support@lunagroupapp.com.au, or WhatsApp concierge (Gold members).

3.2 Collected automatically when you use the app

  • Device info — device type, OS version, app version, language, IP address, crash logs.
  • Usage info — screens visited, buttons tapped, missions attempted, QR codes scanned, push-notification opens.
  • Location — approximate location only when you actively check in at a venue or when you foreground the app inside one of our venue geofences. We do not track your background location.

3.3 Received from third parties

  • Stripe — confirmation that a payment succeeded, the last-4 digits of your card, card brand, country. We never see or store full card numbers.
  • Apple / Google — push-notification tokens so we can send you event alerts and reward receipts.
  • Venue staff at the door — confirmation that a QR check-in or reward ticket was validated.

5. Who we share it with

We share limited personal info only with the following service providers, under contract, for the purposes listed:

  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing and subscription billing. Card data goes directly to Stripe and never touches our servers.
  • MongoDB Atlas (AWS Sydney region) — encrypted database hosting.
  • Apple Push Notification Service & Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivery of push notifications.
  • Apple Wallet & Google Wallet — optional membership pass if you choose to save one.
  • OpenAI / Anthropic / Google (via Emergent LLM routing) — to power the in-app Luna AI concierge. We send anonymised message content only; no name, email or payment data.
  • Venue staff at the door — your first name, last initial, tier, and photo when you present a QR ticket for validation.
  • Law-enforcement or regulators — only when lawfully required by court order, warrant, or under the Privacy Act.

We never sell your personal information. We never share your data with advertisers.

6. Where we store it

Your data is stored on servers located in Sydney, Australia (AWS ap-southeast-2) and replicated to Melbourne for disaster recovery. Stripe stores payment tokens in Australia and the United States. Push-notification token delivery uses Apple (US) and Google (Singapore/US) infrastructure.

7. How long we keep it

  • Active account data — kept while your account is active.
  • After you delete your account — personally identifying fields (name, phone, DOB, address, push token, profile photo) are anonymised immediately and all active subscriptions cancelled. Any residual PII is fully purged within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated analytics may be retained indefinitely.
  • Payment records — retained 7 years for Australian tax law compliance (tied to a redacted user id, not your name).
  • Check-in logs containing your user-id — retained for 2 years for fraud investigation, then deleted.

8. Your rights (and how to use them)

Under the Australian Privacy Principles (and GDPR where applicable) you can:

  • Access the personal info we hold about you — open the app and go to Profile → Settings → Download My Data (tap to receive a JSON export of your full record), or email privacy@lunagroupapp.com.au.
  • Correct any inaccurate info — edit in-app at Profile → Edit or email us.
  • Delete your account and all personal data — in-app at Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Processed within 30 days.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications, marketing emails, or location access at any time from iOS/Android settings or in-app toggles.
  • Lodge a complaint — with us first, or with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

9. How we protect it

  • All network traffic uses TLS 1.2+.
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (minimum cost 12). We cannot read your password.
  • Reward and milestone QR codes are HMAC-SHA256 signed and single-use — they cannot be reused via screenshot.
  • Database encryption at rest (AWS-managed keys).
  • Staff access to production data is role-gated and logged.
  • We run quarterly dependency vulnerability scans.

We'll notify affected users and the OAIC within 30 days of becoming aware of any eligible data breach, as required by the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

10. Cookies and tracking (website)

Our website (lunagroupapp.com.au) uses only essential session cookies. We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any cross-site tracking. The mobile app does not contain any advertising SDKs.

11. Children

The Luna Group app is strictly for users aged 18 or over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has registered, email privacy@lunagroupapp.com.au and we will delete the account immediately.

12. Changes to this policy

If we make a material change we'll notify you in-app and by email at least 14 days before it takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.
